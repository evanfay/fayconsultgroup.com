Our Mission

Practical AI for Companies That Build, Ship, and Move Things

Too many AI consultants sell transformation. We sell results. Fay Consult Group was built specifically for manufacturing, logistics, and supply chain companies in the 20–200 employee range — the companies that run the real economy but rarely get the same technology access as the enterprise players.

We don't parachute in with a framework and leave. We sit down with your ops director, walk your floor, understand your data, and build systems that your team actually uses. That's it. That's the whole model.

Our focus is the PA/NJ/MD/DE corridor — local enough to be on-site when it matters, experienced enough to serve any operation in the region.

Evan Fay

Founder & Principal Consultant

Evan spent years working in lean manufacturing and operations analysis before making the transition into AI consulting — which means he's been in the production meetings, built the tracking spreadsheets, and felt the frustration of a report that takes four hours to pull together every Monday morning. That background isn't incidental. It's the foundation of how Fay Consult Group approaches every engagement.

When he started working with AI tools seriously, the immediate observation was clear: the technology was powerful, but most of the consulting around it was built for people with data science teams and seven-figure technology budgets. The mid-market operations world — the manufacturers, the 3PLs, the distributors — was being largely ignored. Fay Consult Group is the answer to that gap.

Evan works directly with clients on every engagement. No account managers, no junior consultants handed the keys after the kickoff call.

Our Approach

Three Principles Behind
Everything We Build

These aren't marketing words. They're the filters we run every engagement decision through.

01

Practical Over Theoretical

We don't sell you a vision of AI. We build something that runs in your environment, works with your data, and solves a specific problem your team has today. If it can't be deployed and adopted in weeks — not quarters — we rethink the approach.

02

ROI-First

Before we write a single line of code or build a single workflow, we've identified what success looks like in dollars and hours. Every recommendation is tied to a measurable outcome — because that's what you're paying us for, not innovation theater.

03

Built for Mid-Market Operations

We're not a generalist AI shop that dabbles in manufacturing. This is all we do. That means we understand your ERP constraints, your shift-based data patterns, your workforce dynamics, and the difference between what works in a warehouse and what works in a slide deck.
