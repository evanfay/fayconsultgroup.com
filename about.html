Meet Evan Fay

Evan Fay

Founder and Principal Consultant

Evan spent years working in lean manufacturing and operations analysis before transitioning into AI consulting. That means he has been in the production meetings, built the tracking spreadsheets, and felt the frustration of a report that takes four hours to pull together every Monday morning. That background is not incidental. It is the foundation of how Fay Consult Group approaches every engagement.

When he started working seriously with AI tools, the same observation kept coming up. The technology was powerful, but most of the consulting around it was built for companies with data science teams and large technology budgets. The small and mid-size operations world, the manufacturers, the 3PLs, the distributors, was being largely ignored. Fay Consult Group is the answer to that gap.

Evan works directly with every client on every engagement. No account managers. No junior consultants handed the keys after the kickoff call.

Our Approach

Three Principles Behind Everything We Build

These are not marketing words. They are the filters we run every engagement decision through.

01

Practical Over Theoretical

We do not sell you a vision of AI. We build something that runs in your environment, works with your data, and solves a specific problem your team has today. If it cannot be deployed and adopted in weeks, we rethink the approach.

02

ROI First

Before we write a single line of code or build a single workflow, we have identified what success looks like in dollars and hours. Every recommendation is tied to a measurable outcome. That is what you are paying us for, not innovation theater.

03

Built for Small and Mid-Size Operations

We are not a generalist AI shop that dabbles in manufacturing. This is all we do. That means we understand your ERP constraints, your shift-based data patterns, and the difference between what works on a warehouse floor and what works in a slide deck.
